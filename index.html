Lee Jong Suk

About Lee Jong Suk

Lee Jong Suk is a South Korean actor and model. He debuted in 2005 as a runway model, becoming the youngest male model ever to participate in Seoul Fashion Week. He gained widespread fame for his roles in popular dramas such as School 2013, I Can Hear Your Voice, Pinocchio, and While You Were Sleeping.

Career Highlights

  • Birthdate: September 14, 1989
  • Debut: 2005 as a runway model
  • Notable Dramas: School 2013, Pinocchio, W, Romance Is a Bonus Book
  • Awards: Multiple acting awards including the Baeksang Arts Awards
